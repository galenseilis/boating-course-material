LAWS, REGULATIONS AND CODE
Several laws, regulations and code apply to all pleasure craft operators. An operator who breaks one of these laws, regulations and/or code is subject to penalties and/or fines. Here are some of the laws, regulations and code:

Canada Shipping Act 2001;
Contraventions Act;
Vessel Operation Restriction Regulations;
Charts and Nautical Publication Regulations;
Collision Regulations;
Small Vessel Regulations;
Criminal Code of Canada.
For additional information on safe boating please refer to the Transport Canada Safe Boating Guide

AS OF SEPTEMBER 2009 ALL OPERATORS OF A PLEASURE CRAFT MUST HAVE A COMPETENCY CARD.
